# PAS Framework Examples by Industry

This document provides extended PAS (Problem-Agitation-Solution) examples across various industries and use cases. Use these as inspiration when crafting project descriptions.

---

## E-Commerce & Retail

### Subscription Box Service

**Problem:**
*Consumers want to discover new products but face decision paralysis and fear of wasted money on items they won't like. Traditional sampling methods are expensive and limited in variety.*

**Agitation:**
*The result? Missed opportunities for brands to reach new customers and consumers stuck in purchasing ruts, buying the same products repeatedly. Market research shows 73% of consumers express interest in trying new products but cite cost and risk as primary barriers. Brands lose millions in customer acquisition, unable to break through consumer inertia.*

**Solution:**
*Our curated subscription service delivers personalized product samples based on individual preferences and browsing behavior. Subscribers receive 5-8 premium samples monthly, curated by algorithms that match taste profiles with inventory. Customers can try before committing to full-size purchases, reducing risk and increasing discovery. The service has driven a 40% conversion rate from sample to full-size purchase, with brands reporting 3x higher customer lifetime value from subscribers versus one-time buyers.*

## SaaS / B2B Software

### Employee Engagement Platform

**Problem:**
*Companies struggle with low employee engagement, leading to productivity losses and high turnover. Traditional surveys provide infrequent, backward-looking data that fails to capture real-time sentiment.*

**Agitation:**
*The costs are staggering: disengaged employees cost U.S. businesses $450-550 billion annually in lost productivity. Turnover expenses reach 1.5-2x an employee's salary. By the time annual survey results reveal engagement issues, it's too late—top talent has already left. Managers lack the tools to identify and address problems proactively, leading to cascading team dysfunction and culture erosion.*

**Solution:**
*Our platform provides continuous, real-time engagement monitoring through lightweight pulse checks and AI-driven sentiment analysis. Managers receive instant alerts when team sentiment dips, along with actionable recommendations tailored to their specific situation. The system anonymizes responses to encourage honest feedback while aggregating data to identify organization-wide patterns. Clients report a 35% reduction in voluntary turnover within the first year, with 92% of managers stating the platform helped them retain at-risk employees they would have otherwise lost.*

## Healthcare & MedTech

### Telemedicine Platform

**Problem:**
*Rural populations and individuals with mobility challenges face significant barriers accessing specialized healthcare. Patients travel hours for appointments that could be conducted remotely, while specialists remain concentrated in urban centers.*

**Agitation:**
*This access gap leads to delayed diagnoses, worsened health outcomes, and increased healthcare costs. A patient with a chronic condition requiring monthly specialist visits may lose 12 full workdays annually to travel, creating financial strain and treatment non-adherence. Emergency room visits spike when minor issues go untreated due to access barriers. The system fails everyone—patients receive suboptimal care, providers burn out from demand overload, and payers bear the cost of preventable complications.*

**Solution:**
*Our telemedicine platform connects patients with board-certified specialists through secure, HIPAA-compliant video consultations. Patients can schedule same-day appointments from home, eliminating travel barriers and reducing wait times from weeks to hours. The platform integrates with existing electronic health records, ensuring specialists have complete medical histories. Integrated remote monitoring devices allow providers to track vital signs and adjust treatment plans in real-time. Health system partners report a 40% reduction in ER utilization, 60% decrease in no-show rates, and $2.3M in annual savings from prevented hospital readmissions.*

## FinTech & Banking

### Automated Expense Management

**Problem:**
*Small businesses lose 20% of revenue to expense leakage, fraud, and inefficient reimbursement processes. Manual expense tracking is error-prone, time-consuming, and creates friction for employees who front costs and wait weeks for repayment.*

**Agitation:**
*Finance teams spend 40% of their time chasing receipts, reconciling spreadsheets, and investigating policy violations—time that could be spent on strategic work. Employees bear cash flow burdens from delayed reimbursements, leading to dissatisfaction and reduced morale on sales teams. The lack of real-time visibility means budget overruns spiral for weeks before detection. Meanwhile, 15% of expense report dollars are lost to duplicate payments, policy violations, or outright fraud that manual processes fail to catch.*

**Solution:**
*Our platform automates expense management from receipt to reimbursement. Employees simply photograph receipts or forward emails—the system extracts data, matches purchases to corporate cards, enforces policy in real-time, and routes for approval with automatic fraud detection. Reimbursements process in 24 hours instead of 2 weeks. CFOs gain live dashboards showing spend by category, vendor, and employee with predictive budget forecasting. Clients reduce expense processing costs by 70%, recover 12% of spend previously lost to policy violations, and report 95% employee satisfaction with the reimbursement experience.*

## EdTech / Learning

### Corporate Training Platform

**Problem:**
*Companies invest billions in employee training that fails to stick. Traditional learning management systems host content but don't ensure retention or application. Employees complete modules for compliance but forget 80% within 30 days.*

**Agitation:**
*The ROI on training spend evaporates when learning doesn't translate to performance. Sales teams complete negotiation training but don't apply techniques in calls. Safety training gets completed yet accidents persist from protocol failures. The problem isn't content quality—it's the lack of reinforcement, application, and measurement. L&D leaders lack visibility into what's actually working, throwing money at courses without knowing impact. The result: wasted budgets, frustrated employees who see training as a checkbox exercise, and leadership questioning the value of development investments.*

**Solution:**
*Our platform applies spaced repetition and behavioral science to ensure training translates to performance. Content delivery is personalized based on role and skill gaps, with automated refreshers scheduled at optimal intervals for memory retention. Managers receive coaching prompts to reinforce learning in team meetings and 1:1s. The system measures on-the-job application through workflow integrations—tracking whether sales reps use new negotiation tactics or managers apply leadership principles. Analytics dashboards show which courses drive performance improvements, allowing L&D teams to optimize content investment. Clients see 3x improvement in knowledge retention, 67% increase in on-the-job application, and measurable performance gains: +23% in sales metrics, -40% in safety incidents, and 2x faster leader promotion rates.*

## Marketing & AdTech

### Customer Data Platform (CDP)

**Problem:**
*Marketers struggle with fragmented customer data scattered across CRMs, website analytics, email platforms, and social media. Building unified customer profiles requires manual data engineering that most marketing teams lack in-house.*

**Agitation:**
*Without unified profiles, marketers deliver disjointed experiences that frustrate customers. A prospect who abandoned a cart receives a generic email blast instead of a personalized reminder. A VIP customer gets treated like a first-time visitor because website behavior doesn't sync with purchase history. The result: lower conversion rates, damaged customer experience, and wasted ad spend targeting the wrong people or retargeting existing customers. Marketing campaigns fly blind, unable to measure true attribution across touchpoints. CMOs report that 30% of their ad budget is wasted due to poor data quality and segmentation.*

**Solution:**
*Our CDP automatically ingests and unifies customer data from 50+ sources without requiring engineering work. Machine learning algorithms resolve identities across devices and channels, creating single customer views that update in real-time. Marketers build segments using natural language queries—"show me customers who visited pricing twice in the last week but haven't purchased"—and activate audiences instantly across ad platforms, email, and website personalization. The platform maintains compliance with privacy regulations while giving teams self-service access to data previously locked in engineering queues. Clients achieve 45% lift in conversion rates through personalization, reduce acquisition costs by 30% via better targeting, and report saving 20 hours weekly previously spent on manual data pulls and spreadsheet wrangling.*

## ClimateTech & Sustainability

### Carbon Footprint Tracking for SMBs

**Problem:**
*Small and medium businesses want to reduce their environmental impact but lack tools to measure emissions. Enterprise carbon accounting solutions are too expensive and complex for companies without dedicated sustainability teams.*

**Agitation:**
*The result: inaction on climate commitments. Companies set net-zero goals publicly but have no way to track progress or identify reduction opportunities. They miss out on business from enterprise customers requiring supplier sustainability metrics. Employees demand climate action but leadership lacks data to prioritize initiatives. Meanwhile, SMBs collectively represent 50% of business emissions—their inaction significantly impacts global climate goals. Without accessible measurement, the vast majority of businesses remain excluded from the sustainability transition.*

**Solution:**
*Our platform makes carbon accounting accessible for any business without requiring sustainability expertise. Companies simply connect accounting software and utility accounts—the system automatically calculates emissions from spend data using established methodologies and emission factors. The platform identifies reduction opportunities by benchmarking against similar businesses and highlighting high-impact actions. Automated sustainability reports satisfy customer requests and investor ESG requirements. The system also connects businesses to green vendors and incentive programs for emissions-reducing investments. Users achieve an average 18% emissions reduction in year one through targeted efficiency upgrades, with 60% of users reporting new business wins from meeting customer sustainability requirements.*

## Key Takeaways

Across all industries, effective PAS examples share these elements:

1. **Specific, quantified problems** - Never vague
2. **Emotional + logical agitation** - Appeal to both heart and mind
3. **Solutions focused on outcomes** - What users achieve, not how it works
4. **Measurable business impact** - Revenue, time saved, performance gains
5. **External perspective** - Written for the beneficiary, not the builder

Adapt these patterns to your specific project while maintaining outcome-focused language.
